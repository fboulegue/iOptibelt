  • optibelt TT optical

    After switching on the device, it is ready for immediate measurement. The mounted and tensioned belt is made to vibrate by finger pressure or by hitting it with something.
    The measuring head has to be held above the belt to function, the TT optical starts the measurement and displays the result in Hertz [Hz].
    The state, colour and type of the belt do not influence the measurement, since the measurement is based on an optical signal.
