  • optibelt TT optical

    The Optibelt TT optical frequency tension tester uses vibration frequency as a means of measuring belt tension accurately. This newly developed device provides universal application possibilities for drives in mechanical engineering, the automotive industry and many other application areas. The initial tension values of V-belts, kraftbands, ribbed belts, and timing belts can be tested easily and rapidly.

    The TT optical has even more advantages:
    • easy handling
    • measurement range 5 Hz - 500 Hz
    • compact size
    • highest reliability
    • high measuring accuracy
    • factory calibrated
    • CE approved
