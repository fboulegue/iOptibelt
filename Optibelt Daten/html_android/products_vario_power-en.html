  • optibelt VARIO POWER

    The base compound consists of a polychloroprene rubber with fibres inlaid transversely to the running direction. The high quality and extremely low-stretch tension cord of polyester or aramid is embedded into a rubber cushion compound. It is effectively supported by a fabric outer surface and by the base compound. The base compound provided with its incorporated transverse fibres provides transverse rigidity without sacrificing flexibility.

    Advantages
    • high power ratings
    • long service life
    • smooth running even at high speeds
    • high flexibility
    • optimised heat dissipation

    Optibelt VARIO POWER variable speed belts are preferably used for infinitely variable speed control. The special belt structure allows high dynamic loads, a superior power transmission capability and good control characteristics.
    Also available as double-sided belt.
